✦ Attractions

Thrills for Everyone

From heart-pumping slides to relaxing lazy rivers — there's something magical for every age.

🌊

Wave Pool

Dive into our massive wave pool with 6-foot waves! Feel the ocean experience right here in the park.

🔥 Most Popular
🎢

Tornado Twister

Pakistan's tallest water slide! A 60-foot drop at 50km/h will leave you breathless and screaming for more.

⚡ Extreme Thrill
🛟

Lazy River

Drift along our 400-meter scenic lazy river. Grab a float and just relax under the warm sun.

😌 Relaxing
🏄

Surf Simulator

Learn to surf on our state-of-the-art FlowRider machine. Lessons available for beginners and pros!

🌟 New This Year
🐬

Dolphin Splash Zone

A fun-filled kids area with mini slides, splash pads, and water cannons. Safe and exciting for little ones!

👶 Kids Friendly
🏊

Olympic Pool

Our Olympic-size swimming pool with crystal-clear water. Perfect for laps or casual swimming sessions.

💧 All Ages

✦ Why Choose Us

Your Safety & Fun
is Our Priority

We combine world-class infrastructure with exceptional hospitality to give every visitor the best aquatic experience in Pakistan.

🛡️

100% Safety Certified

All rides and pools are inspected daily. Trained lifeguards stationed at every point in the park.

🌿

Eco-Friendly Water Systems

Advanced water recycling and filtration technology keeps our pools clean and eco-responsible.

🍔

Food Courts & Restaurants

6 dining spots serving Pakistani and international cuisines. From BBQ to ice cream!

🅿️

Free Parking & Easy Access

Massive parking area with dedicated lanes. Located on the main highway for easy travel.
